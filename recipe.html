CREAMY STRAWBERRY SMOOTHIE

ingredients:

  1. Frozen strawberries
  2. Fresh Banana
  3. Greek Youghurt
  4. Ice
  5. Maple syrup
  6. milk

Method

  • Place all ingredients in a blender
  • break the banana into pieces
  • Blend until creamy and frothy
  • stop and scrap down the sides as necessary.
  • If desired, garnish with a frozen strawberry and mint sprig.
  • Serve immediately or store in a covered jar in the refrigerator for 2 days.
